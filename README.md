Hi 👋 My name is Raj Kanjariya
=====================================================================================================================================

Healthcare Technology Researcher | Project Scientist | Unity XR Developer
-------------------------------

I work at the intersection of healthcare XR, 3D medical image visualization, brain-computer interfaces, and neurorehabilitation robotics. My current work focuses on building practical systems for surgical planning, immersive simulation, assistive communication, and next-generation medical interaction platforms.

* 🌍  Based in India
* 🏥  Currently working on **VOXELLENCE** — a 3D medical imaging and volumetric visualization platform
* 🧠  Developing a portable **cVEP speller** for bedside communication
* 🥽  Building healthcare XR systems and Meta Quest multi-device workflows
* 🤖  Exploring neurorehabilitation robotics and 3DOF/6DOF rehabilitation systems
* 🌱  Learning **VTK.js** and advanced medical visualization workflows
